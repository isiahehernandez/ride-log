HOW TO EXPORT YOUR ACTIVITIES
🟠 STRAVA

Open activity → ⋯ menu → Export GPX. Or bulk export via Settings → My Account → Download your Data.

⌚ APPLE WATCH

Use the free Health Export app (App Store) to export workouts as GPX, or share directly from the Fitness app.

🔵 GARMIN

Garmin Connect → activity → Export → Export GPX or Export Original (FIT).

📱 OTHER

Most apps (Wahoo, Komoot, Zwift, Ride with GPS) offer GPX export from any activity detail page.
